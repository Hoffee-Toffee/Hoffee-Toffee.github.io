Dead Space

I love the Dead Space series not for the horror; but for the rich design of the game.

As a horror game, it relies on players feeling as if the events are happening to them, which is why realistic and immersive design is so important.

Dead Space's sci-fi setting builds perfectly on this, adding to the isolation and wonder of the game.

The sci-fi aspect is used for the diagetic UI, with elements such as...

  • Health meters inspired by diving suit oxygen meters.

  • Menus displayed through suit operating system via a chest-mounted holographic projector.

  • Navigation paths displayed via a wrist-mounted holographic projector.

  • Ammo counters displayed on the weapons themselves.

Artificial Intelligence

AI is a rapidly advancing and highly misunderstood field.

I love AI due to it's potential to improve humanity, and I am excited to see what the future holds.

Like any new technology, people are scared of it's potential, imagining senarios as if society would remain static as AI advances.

AI is a powerful technology that has the power to harm and help humanity, which is why regulation and ethics are so important.

Here are some of my favourite AI companies and projects...

  • Neuralink, using AI to decode and encode signals from the brain, allowing for brain-computer interfaces.
  • OpenAI, their AI ethics and safety is great, allowing for the safe development of AI.
  • Tesla, using AI to create general purpose robots and self-driving cars.
  • Latitude, creating AI powered games to enhance the gaming experience.
