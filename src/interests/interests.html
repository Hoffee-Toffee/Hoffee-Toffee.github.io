Dead Space

I love the Dead Space series for the rich design of the game.

As a horror game, it relies on players feeling as if the events are happening to them, which is why immersive audio and UI design is so important.

Dead Space's terrifyingly beautiful sci-fi setting builds perfectly on this, adding to the isolation and wonder of the game.

The sci-fi aspect is used for the diagetic UI, with elements such as...

  • Health meters inspired by diving suit oxygen meters.

  • Menus displayed through suit operating system via a chest-mounted holographic projector.

  • Navigation paths displayed via a wrist-mounted holographic projector.

  • Ammo counters displayed on the weapons themselves.
