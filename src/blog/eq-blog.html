EQ

What is Emotional Intelligence?

All about understanding of emotions, including finding root causes, expressing, controlling your emotions, and understanding others.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ is all about how you generally approach problems, while EQ is centered around only how you understand and deal with emotions.

Why is EQ important?

Emotions are not generally rational, but they are logical
As complex as they can be, they are not random

Understanding your own situation or that of others is powerful to working together well
Even simply understanding that emotions are logical can help you accept, but not always agree with them
This is usually the most crucial element, as it is always the first step in working with the emotions of yourself and others
